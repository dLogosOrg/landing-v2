Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I see results after launching on dLogos? +

Most podcasters see initial fan nominations within 24-48 hours of announcing dLogos to their audience. Meaningful guest booking leverage typically develops within 2-3 weeks as nominations accumulate. The key is actively promoting the platform to your existing listeners across multiple episodes and social channels.

What size audience do I need to make dLogos effective? +

dLogos is designed for growth-stage podcasters with 500+ monthly downloads. While smaller shows can benefit, the social proof effect becomes compelling around 1,000+ monthly downloads when you can generate 50+ nominations for popular guests. The platform scales with your audienceâ€”larger shows see proportionally more momentum.

How do I verify that nominations represent real fan demand? +

dLogos uses verified fan accounts to prevent manipulation. Fans must connect through their podcast app or social media to nominate guests. We track voting patterns and use AI to detect fraudulent activity. Each nomination is tied to a real listener profile, giving you and potential guests confidence in the authenticity of demand.

Can I control which guests get nominated on my show? +

Yes, you maintain full editorial control. You can set guidelines for nominations, moderate submissions before they go live, and remove inappropriate nominations. Many creators find that fan suggestions often align with their show's direction while introducing fresh perspectives they hadn't considered.

How does guest outreach work with dLogos? +

dLogos provides templated outreach messages that include verified fan demand metrics, nomination counts, and compiled fan testimonials. You can customize these templates to match your voice. The platform also suggests optimal timing for outreach based on nomination momentum and provides contact information when available.

What happens if a guest declines despite high fan demand? +

High fan demand doesn't guarantee bookings, but it significantly improves your chances. When guests decline, you can use the nomination data to find similar guests in the same field. The accumulated fan interest often transfers to alternative guests, and you can leverage the demonstrated audience appetite for that topic or expertise area.

Does dLogos cost anything? What's the business model? +

dLogos is free for podcasters to set up and use. We operate on a success-based modelâ€”we only benefit when you successfully book guests and grow your audience. Advanced analytics and premium features are available for established shows looking to maximize their growth potential.

How does dLogos help with audience growth beyond guest booking? +

When fans nominate guests, they naturally share those nominations with their networks to build support. This viral effect introduces new listeners to your show before episodes are even recorded. Additionally, the anticipation around potential guest appearances keeps your audience engaged between episodes, reducing churn and increasing word-of-mouth recommendations.
