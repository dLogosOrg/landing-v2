Frequently Asked Questions

What is dLogos? +

dLogos is the first audience-participation platform that turns podcast guest selection into a public, trackable movement. We empower fans to nominate dream guests, rally their communities, and shape the conversations that matter.

How does the nomination process work? +

Connect your X account, browse partnered podcasts, and nominate your dream guest. Share your nomination to gather supporters. When nominations reach critical mass, podcasts take notice and conversations happen. Every supporter is verified to ensure authentic community backing.

Do podcasts actually listen to nominations? +

Yes! We've facilitated 437+ successful nominations that became real episodes. Our partnered podcasts actively monitor nominations because they value authentic fan input. High-vote nominations create undeniable social proof that helps hosts book dream guests.

How do I become a Dialogue Champion? +

Start nominating guests and sharing your picks. As your nominations gain support and some become real episodes, you'll earn recognition, badges, and influence within the community. Top champions get early access to new features and direct input on platform development.

Is it free to participate? +

Yes! Creating nominations, voting, and participating in the community is completely free. We believe in democratizing podcast curation and making it accessible to all fans who want to shape culture.
